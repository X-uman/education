### Details
1. Puncture (percutaneously or open)
2. Aspiration
3. Instillation
	- Hypertonic saline: 20%
	- 0.5% cetrimide with 0.05% chlorhexidine
	- 10% Povidone iodine
	- Absolute alcohol
4. Re-aspiration

### Contraindications
- Peripherally located cyst
- Inaccessible cyst
- Multiloculated cyst
- Cystobiliary communication
- Cysts in lungs and brain: No pericyst in [[Hydatid cyst]] 
- Calcified cyst

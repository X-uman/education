- Lead accumulation in renal cells 
- Lead damaging the tubulo-Interstitium of kidney and decrease Uric acid excretion: Gouty symptoms and Azotemia
- [[Hyperuricemia]] out of proportion to fall in [[GFR]] 
- other features 
	- [[CKD]], [[Renal atrophy]] 
	- [[Hypertension]]

- take within 1 h if:
	- GCS < 13 
	- FND
	- Suspected [[Skull fractures]]
	- > 1 episode of vomiting
	- Seizure after trauma

- take within 8 h
	- Old age > 65 y
	- [[Coagulation panel]] disorder e.g. aspirin
	- Dangerous mechanism of injury
	- Retrograde amnesia > 30 min (moderate severity)

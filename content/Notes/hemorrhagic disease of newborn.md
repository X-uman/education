### Etiology
- [[Vitamin K deficiency]]
- Maternal [[Anti-Epileptic therapy]]
- Maternal [[Warfarin]] intake
### Clinical features

| Early onset                                                             | Classic                                                               | Late onset                                                                                                                       |

| in first 24 h                                                           | in 2nd to 7th day                                                     | Occurs in 2nd -12th week                                                                                                         |

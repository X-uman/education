### Presentation
- [[Patterned hair loss]] 
- Miniature hair: Shortened anagen phase
- Increased vellous hair, reduced terminal hair

### Management
- Topical
	- [[minoxidil]] 2% in females, 5% for males
- Systemic
	- [[5-alpha reductase inhibitors]] 
	- [[Antiandrogenic drugs]] in females
- [[Hair transplantation]]

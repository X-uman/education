- [[Hypothalamic stress response]] 

### Effects
- Increases
	- Appetite
	- [[Gluconeogenesis]]
	- [[Glycogenesis]]
	- [[Protein catabolism]]
	- [[Lipolysis]] and [[Hyperlipidemia]] 
	- [[Insulin resistance]]
- Decreases
	- [[Protein synthesis]] leading to [[Osteoporosis]], Easy bruising, [[delayed wound healing]] 
	- [[immunocompromised status]]
	- Permissive effects on [[Catecholamines]]

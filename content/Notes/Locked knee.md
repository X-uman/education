### Really locked i.e. True locking
- Something inside the joint
	- Torn meniscus
	- Cartilage fragments
	- Bone fragments
- Presentation
	- Snapping sensation 
	- Feel a bump at the site of loose body
	- Intermittent pain 

### Not really locked, due to muscle spasm
- Swelling
- Inflammation
- Patellar maltracking
- [[Plica syndrome]]
- Knee injury

#### Presentation
- Feeling of knee catching

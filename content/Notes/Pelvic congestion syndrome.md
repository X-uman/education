### Clinical features
- Congestive [[dysmenorrhea]] without demonstrable pelvic pathology
- Menorrhagia and epimenorrhea
- Uterus may feel bulky and boggy

### Diagnostics
- Pelvic venography

### managemenet
- Medroxy progesterone acetate 50 mg daily for 4 months
- Hysterectomy

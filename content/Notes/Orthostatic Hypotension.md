- Ask patient to lie supine/sit for about 1 min
- Take BP
- Then ask them to stand up
	- Wait 1 min
	- Take BP
	- Take BP after 3 min of standing

When to say they have orthostatic hypotension
- When SBP falls by > 20
- Or DBP falls by > 10
- Or patients feels lightheaded

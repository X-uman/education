### Possible symptoms
- Gum bleeding
- Pus from gums
- Bad smell (halitosis)
- Jaw pain, radiating to face, or ears
- Deformities
	- Swelling
	- Malocclusions
	- Facial deformities
	- Pathological fractures
- Numbness 
- Teeth loosening

#### Systemic symptoms
- Fever
- Weight loss

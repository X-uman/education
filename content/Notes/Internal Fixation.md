### Indications
- Unstable fracture not held by non-operable means
- Fractures requiring rigid immobilization
- After open reduction, especially if associated with neurovascular injury
### [[implants]] used in IF
- [[Nails]]
- [[Plates]]
- [[Screws]]
- [[Wires]] 

### Complications
1. Infection
2. [[fracture nonunion]]
3. [[Implant failure]]
4. Refracture

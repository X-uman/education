### Causes
- Forceful retraction in [[Phimosis]]
#### Management
- Non-surgical 
	- Manual reduction
	- Hypertonic saline
	- Multiple small punctures to allow oozing of edema fluids
	- Ice packing

- Surgery 
	- Dorsal slit followed by [[Circumscision]]

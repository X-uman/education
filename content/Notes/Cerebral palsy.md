### Management
- Multidisciplinary approach
- [[Physiotherapy]]
- [[Skeletal Muscle relaxants]] 
- [[Psychotherapy]] 
- [[Speech therapy]]

### Differential diagnoses
- Hypothyroid myopathy: [[Cretinism]] 
- [[Mitochondrial myopathies]] 
- [[Progressive muscular dystrophies]]
- [[Myelomeningocele]]
- Traumatic peripheral nerve lesions
- Genetic syndromes

### ADRS
- [[Vagolytic effects]] due to steroid group of releaxants (have suffix Onium)
- [[Histamine]]
- Stress on heart increased
- Analogy: [[Myasthenia gravis]] like effects 
### Reversal
- IM [[Neostigmine]]

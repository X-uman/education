### Clinical features
- **Acute toxicity**
	- Nausea, vomiting
	- Vertigo
	- Blurred vision
- **Chronic toxicity**
	- [[Alopecia]]
	- Arthralgias
	- Dry skin, scaling
	- Hepatosplenomegaly, [[Hepatotoxicity]] 
	- [[Idiopathic intracranial hypertension]] 
- **Teratogenic effects**
	- microphthalmia; cleft palate
	- [[Thymus]] agenesis 
	- Cardiovascular abnormalities
	- Safe upper limit: ~10,000 IU daily

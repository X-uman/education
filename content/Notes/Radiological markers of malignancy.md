### USG
- Solid hypoechoic nodule or solid hypoechoic component with at least one of the following:
    - Irregular margins
    - Taller-than-wide shape
    - [[Microcalcifications]] 
    - Rim calcifications with extruding tissue
    - Extrathyroidal extension

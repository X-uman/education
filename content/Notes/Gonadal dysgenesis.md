### Classification
- [[Turner syndrome]] (45,XO)
- Pure gonadal dysgenesis (or complete gonadal dysgenesis)
- 46,XY gonadal dysgenesis ([[Swyer syndrome]])
- 46,XX gonadal dysgenesis
### Clinical features
- [[Delayed puberty]]
- [[Subfertility]]
- Streak gonads
- Malignancies like [[Dysgerminoma]]

### Classification
- Congenital
- Traumatic
- Inflammatory
- Malignant 

### Examination
- Inspection: Number, discharge, changes in opening and surroundings
- Palpation: Tenderness, wall of sinus, mobility (fixed in [[Osteomyelitis]]), lump
- Probe examination for depth and direction 
- Lymphatic drainage

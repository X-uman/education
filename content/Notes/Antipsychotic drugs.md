| ------------------------------------- | --------------------------------- | ----------------------------------- |
| MOA                                   | Dopamine (D2 blockade)            | Dopamine and serotonin blockade     |

| [[Extrapyramidal movement disorders]] | Prominent                         | Less present                        |

| [[Neuroleptic malignant syndrome]]    | Present                           | Present, but less likely            |

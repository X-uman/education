### Variants
- [[Pemphigus erythematosus]] (variant) which resembles [[SLE]] 
- Fogo selvegum: endemic to Brazil
- Drug induced pemphigus (PCR)
	- [[Penicillamine]] due to thiol group
	- Captopril
	- [[Rifampicin]]
	- NSIADs, like Piroxicam
	- [[Phenylbutazone]] 
### Presentation
- Like PV, but mucosa not involved

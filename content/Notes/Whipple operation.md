## Whipple operation 
### Resection 
- Head of the pancreas
- Entire duodenum
- Distal stomach (antrum and pylorus)
- proximal jejunum (15 cm)
- Gall bladder and the CBD

### Anastomoses
- Pancreaticojejunostomy
- Hepaticojejunostomy
- Gastrojejunostomy
- Enteroenterostomy

### General points
- Cerebral blood volume = core = DWI
- Mean transit time = core + Reversible ischemia
- Mismatch = MTT - CBV =  reversible ischemia
- Time to peak 
- Time to drain 
![[Pasted image 20240227175023.png]]
### Normal parameters
- gray matter
    - MTT: 4 s
    - CBF: 60 mL/100 g/min (infarct =  < 30 )
    - CBV: 4 mL/100 g
- white matter
    - MTT: 4.8 s
    - CBF: 25 mL/100 g/min
    - CBV: 2 mL/100 g

### Indications in EDH, More than 
- 30cc clot 
 - 5mm shift of brain
- 1.5cm thickness of hematoma

### Indications in infratentorial hemorrhage
- Large hematoma (> 3 cm)
- Declining neurological status
- Hydrocephalus
- Signs of brain herniation (e.g., Cushing triad)

### Diagnosis
- Infants and children who are 6-59 months of age
- [[MUAC]] < 11.5 cm
- Weight-for-height/length < - 3 Z score
### Management
- Stabilization
- Transition: [[F-100]] 
- Rehabilitation
### Complications during rehabilitation
- [[Idiopathic intracranial hypertension]]
- [[Nutritional recovery syndrome]] 
- [[Refeeding syndrome]]

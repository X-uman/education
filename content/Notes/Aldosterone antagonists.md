### Indications
- All patients with ACC/AHA stage C HFrEF without contraindications i.e., eGFR < 30 mL/min/1.73 m2 and serum K+ > 5.0 mEq/L
- [[Hyperaldosteronism]]
- [[Hypokalemia]]
- Diastolic heart failure
- Drug resistant [[Hypertension]]

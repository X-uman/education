### Diagnostics
- [[Hypergammaglobinemia]] (IgG dominant) 
- Liver biopsy
	- Lymphoplasmacytic interface hepatitis: ongoing inflammatory process with lymphocytic infiltration, bridging or multiacinar necrosis, and fibrotic changes
